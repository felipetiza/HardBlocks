###keyboard shortcuts

| Key | Function |
|------|---------------|
| <kbd>Pad num 1</kbd> | Mapa 1
| <kbd>Pad num 2</kbd> | Mapa 2
| <kbd>Pad num 3</kbd> | Empty Map
| <kbd>Pad num 7</kbd> | information
| <kbd>Enter</kbd> | Pause
| <kbd>Z</kbd> | Weapon 1
| <kbd>X</kbd> | Weapon 2
| <kbd>M</kbd> | Fullscreen
